Our Story!

CREATIVE PRODUCTION AGENCY. The Wav.format is an independent creative house solving branding and content Production has evolved from a small team of passionate filmmakers to Lagos' premier creative studio. We blend traditional African storytelling with cutting-edge cinematography to create visual experiences that resonate across cultures.

Our team of directors, cinematographers, editors, and creative directors work collaboratively to bring your vision to life with a distinct Afro-urban aesthetic that captures the energy and vibrancy of contemporary African culture.
